Why choose Ritu Selects?

Because we understand fashion & clothing!

We do not sell catalog items or low quality Bollywood replicas that flood the online retail market. Neither do we distribute items manufactured at bulk that looks good in images but lacks the detailing you need to look good when you actually wear them.

At Ritu Selects – India Inspired Couture we believe in keeping it simple yet sleek-chic and glamorous that works for today's generation!

Good fashion starts from quality fabrics and intricate craftsmanship, there is no second way around it! Over the years we have managed to create a team that understands this and is consistently able to create and offer high-quality designer wear. We are able to offer great prices by reducing waste and creating products that sell!

Our shopping experience is an extension of our business philosophy, "You buy products when You feel it looks good on You" No gimmicks, No pushy sales (our associates do not get bonus/commission based on sales). We offer a unique and relaxed shopping experience at our showroom and are happy to provide honest fashion expertise while keeping your budget and taste in mind.

We take pride in providing high-quality Bridal & Prom wears, Anarkali Suits, Lehengas, Sarees, Ready-to-wear Blouses, Trendy Tunics & Leggings, Men’s Sherwanis, Kidswear, Made-to-Order custom & fusion wear. We also offer a range of fashion accessories like modern and traditional Earrings, Necklaces, Bangles/Kadas/Bracelets/Clutches, etc.

Check our latest updates on Facebook.com/RituSelects or visit one of our local Showrooms. We ship all over USA and also showcase our products frequently at exhibitions and fashion events.

Last but not the least, At Ritu Selects we believe "Our success is when a client feels beautiful and confident wearing our outfits!"
